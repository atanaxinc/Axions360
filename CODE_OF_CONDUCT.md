# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Axions360 is built for Africa and the world. We are committed to maintaining a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive community for contributors of all backgrounds, nationalities, and experience levels.

We do not tolerate harassment, discrimination, or disrespectful behavior of any kind.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Communicate respectfully and constructively
- Give and receive feedback professionally
- Acknowledge different perspectives and experiences
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community
- Show empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery related to race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, nationality, or age
- Publishing private information about others without consent
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Any behavior that would be considered inappropriate in a professional context

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, email communications, and any other forum where contributors interact as part of the Axions360 community.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to **conduct@atanax.com**.

All reports will be reviewed confidentially. The project maintainers will respond as follows:

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Minor or first offense | Written warning |
| Repeated or significant offense | Temporary ban from project spaces |
| Severe offense | Permanent ban |
